Teaching

I served as a teaching assistant for some undegraduate courses at USP: Calculus II for Engineering, Differentiable Functions and Series, Real Analysis for Math Education and Introduction to Algebraic Topology.

Other teaching and outreach

In all of my undergraduate years I participated at the S4 seminar, which is a student run seminar for freshmen, whose goal is to introduce advanced Mathematics in a simpliefied way. In 2021, I started an academic group, the A5 Group, wich organizes seminars and events for Undergraduate and Master students. Our aim is to introduce research level math for students at IME.
